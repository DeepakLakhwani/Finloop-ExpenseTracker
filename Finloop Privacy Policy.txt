FinLoop Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 26, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how FinLoop collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use the application.

1. Information We Collect
Personal Financial Information

FinLoop may store:

Transactions
Categories
Account names
Balances
Notes
Budget records

This information is stored solely for app functionality.

Device Information

We may collect:

Device type
Operating system version
App version
Crash logs

for improving app stability and performance.

Authentication Information

If enabled by the user:

Biometric authentication
Passcode settings

are securely handled using device-level security systems.

FinLoop does NOT store raw fingerprint or Face ID data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

Provide finance tracking features
Generate analytics
Sync notes and records
Improve app performance
Secure user accounts
Enable export/import functionality
3. Data Storage
Local Storage

Most financial information is stored locally on the user's device.

Cloud Synchronization

Certain features such as:

Scratchpad notes
Optional backups
Sync services

may use secure cloud infrastructure such as Firebase Firestore.

4. Data Sharing

FinLoop does NOT sell user data.

We do NOT share personal financial information with third parties except:

When required by law
When necessary for app functionality
With secure infrastructure providers
5. Data Security

We implement:

Secure local storage
Encrypted authentication methods
Device-level biometric protection
Secure Firebase communication

However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

6. Excel Import & Export

Users can:

Export financial data to Excel files
Share exported files externally

Users are responsible for protecting exported files shared outside the app.

7. Notifications

FinLoop may send:

Financial reminders
App-related alerts
Local notifications

Users can disable notifications anytime through settings.

8. Biometric Authentication

Biometric authentication is optional.

Fingerprint and Face ID data are processed only through the device operating system and are never directly accessed or stored by FinLoop.

9. Children's Privacy

FinLoop is not intended for children under 13 years of age.

We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. User Rights

Users may:

Delete app data
Disable cloud synchronization
Remove passcodes
Export their data
Uninstall the application anytime
11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Users are encouraged to review this page regularly.

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy:
